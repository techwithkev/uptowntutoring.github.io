About Us

Kevin

Hi, my name is Kevin. By day I am a professional nerd, helping major organizations such as banks, department stores and telecoms bring their software to customers more efficiently. However, on the weekends and after work I help students reach their full potential by helping them see math and programming in a new way.

I've been tutoring since 2004 in subjects ranging from elementary level maths to business statics, web design to mobile app design. Many of my students are now in top Canadian and US universities. My classes have always been about building knowledge and having fun in the process, when you're having fun, knowledge stays and time flies.

OUR COURSES

Curriculum Mathematics

Reinforce lessons learned in school and stay ahead of the curve! Course contents follow the general Ontario curriculum guide. The focus will be on mastering the basic concepts of maths to build a firm foundation moving forward.

Competition Mathematics

Prepare for matematical competitions held by Mathematica, American Mathematics Competitions(AMC) and the University of Waterloo. This fosters critical thinking skills and also provides an edge when applying for more competitive schools. You will learn how basic concepts in maths can be combined in particular sequences to solve more complex problems along with test-taking techniques and hints.

Web Design

Unleash your creativity! Our lives now revolve around the internet so what better thing to learn than how the internet works and at the same time make something both beautiful and practical? You will learn what is the internet, how websites such as this one is made and create your own masterpiece that will be put online for everyone to admire.

Computer Programming
